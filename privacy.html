POCKET STRUM

Privacy policy · effective RELEASE DATE

Privacy

Pocket Strum collects no data. None.

No analytics, no advertising, no accounts, no tracking, and no network connection is made by the app.

What the app does with information

Everything you do in Pocket Strum — the chords you play, your fader settings, your chosen look — stays on your device. Settings are stored locally so the app remembers them; they are never transmitted anywhere.

Microphone, contacts, location

The app requests no permissions. It does not access the microphone, camera, contacts, photos or location.

Purchases

The app is sold through Apple's App Store. Apple processes the payment; we receive no personal or payment information about you. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and, since it collects no data at all, it collects no data from children.

Changes

If a future version ever changes any of the above (for example, an optional feature that needs a permission), this policy will be updated first and the change described in the App Store release notes.
